samādhisaṃbhāraparivartaḥlokanāthāya namaḥbodhicittaṃ dṛḍhaṃ kuryāt pūrva kāruṇyaśaktijam|bhavabhogasukhe'saktaḥ
parigrahaparāṅmukhaḥ

śraddhādidhanasampanno guruṃ buddhasamaṃ bhajet|taddiṣṭasamayācāraṃ paripālitumudyataḥ

kumbhaguhyābheṣekaṃ tu prasādāllabhate guroḥ|kāyavāk cittasaṃśuddhaḥ siddhipātraṃ sa sādhakaḥ

samādhyaṅgasamudbhūta- saṃbhārapāripūritaḥ|śīghraṃ siddhyāptirevaṃ ca guhyamantranayasthitiḥ

samādhyaṅgacyutau tasya vipakṣasthititastathā|janmakoṭisahasraiśca samādhirna prasidhyati

tatsamādhiprasidhyartha guhyavṛttasthayogavit|tad bhinnaṃ cintanaṃ tyaktvā dṛḍhavīryeṇa codyamet

samādheryāni cāṅgāni vipakṣastadviniścaye|samādhyaṅgaṃ vipakṣaśca saṃkṣepeṇa samucyate

śīlasampattiyuktatvaṃ bhogeṣu nirapekṣatā|kṣāntirdṛḍhapratijñā ca janasaṃsargavarjanam

kāyavāk cittakāryeṣu samprajanyasamanvayaḥ|buddhakāyastutismṛtyā kāyānusmṛtisaṃyutiḥ

yasmin kāle tu yatkārya dārḍhrya tasya hyanusmṛtau|pañcāvaraṇahāniśca yuktakṛd bhaktamānatā

samastalokadharmeṣu cittopekṣā sadā tathā|saṃlekho'ṅgāni cemāni vipakṣastadviparyayaḥ

samādhisambhārakṛtairhi puṇyaiḥ samādhiṃ vajropamametu lokaḥ |12, a ba|samādhisambhāraparivarto
mahāpaṇḍitācāryadīpaṅkaraśrījñānapādaviracitaḥ samāptaḥ||tenaiva bhāratīyopādhyāyena mahāsaṃśodhakalokacakṣuṣā
bhikṣu-śākyamatinā ca anūdya nirṇītaḥ||
